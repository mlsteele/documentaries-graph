Definitions of a Documentary

Art vs Reality: Depict of reality without artistic flourish.
Factual: Docs must be factual.
Social/Political: Offer a soc/pol angle.
Contradiction: Contradictory definitions.
Stories: Tell a story.
Technology: Use particular tech.
A Message: Convey a message.
Modes: Docs use defined modes.
Strict definition: Strictly defined.
Idealism: Docs are an abstract ideal.
Interpretation: Eye of the beholder / author.
Loose Definition: Not well defined.
Not Deliberate: Exploration without angled intention.
Observation: Observe without interrupting.
